Why Multibox Paper & Pack

STRENGTHS & ACHIEVEMENTS

Having set-up a state-of-the-art most modern plant with futuristic features such as automatic manufacturing machinery and solar power, Multibox has set a benchmark in business. Core strengths are technical and mechanical superiority, multiple product lines, quality policy, human relation policy, business affiliations and qualified manpower with very strong focus on People before Profit makes Multibox stand out in the field. Business Policy driven by spirit of 3Cs (co-operation, collaboration, commitment), Clients are encouraged to share and discuss their needs and requirements so as to secure highest efficiency and effectiveness for maximum consumer satisfaction.

Business Approach

Multibox promotes an unique model of business based on collaborative partnership where a great deal of time, energy and intellect is invested in client relationship management. This fosters a bond that differentiates Multibox from rest of industry players primarily focused on transactional relationships. We believe in transformational relationships that brings win-win gains for all stake holders in business. We encourage our clients to visit and see the Multibox facility and plant. Sit down with our team to discuss and work-out most efficient, effective and profitable time-cost deliverables to leverage and achieve harmony in supply chain management.
