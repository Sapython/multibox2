Management Team

Multibox being a wholly owned enterprise had the advantage of a long standing business experience of the owners. Management team comprises of professionally qualified specialists in distinct fields of the business. Individual Directors look after their specific KRAs and ensure necessary synergy and operational efficiency.

Mr. Murari Lal Agarwalla, Chairman & Managing Director

A Fellow of Institute of Chartered Accountants of India, Mr Murari Lal Agarwalla is a financial expert and consultant at Pitampura, Delhi. With over thirty years of expertise as a prominent consultant to country’s leading business houses, Mr. Agarwalla’s accomplishments stand out as a testimony of personal power, professionalism, determination and hard work. He began his career as a faculty in Dibrugarh University, Assam, an interest which has now become his lifelong passion. Ex-Proprietor of M/s M.L. Singhi & Associates, a well established CA Firm, M L Agarwalla is known in the business industry for his specialization in finance sector including asset building, corporate communication and public relations besides institutional management. As a Core finance professional, his primary interests lie in asset building, institutional financing and as an educationist and social visionary. As a Founder and Member of Board of Directors, Shri Agarwalla is guiding many social institutions such as:

  • Ekal Vidyalaya
  • Vanvasi Raksha Parivar Foundation (Shri Hari Satsang Samiti)
  • Maharaja Agrasen Hospital, Rohini, New Delhi
  • Mr. Agarwalla is also the Member of Gyan Jyoti Education Foundation which is successfully managing DPS Khanapara, Meghalaya.
  • Trustee of Manas International School, Dehradun, Uttrakhand.
Mr. Mohit Agarwalla, Director (Operations)

A professionally qualified young entrepreneur, Mohit Agarwalla is key management personnel responsible for plant operation including manufacturing and sales. An MBA from Indian Institute of Planning and Management, Delhi and Graduate from Christ University, Bangalore, he had impressive intellectual, professional and managerial credentials. Directly overseeing the day-to-day operations including Quality and HR Departments, Mohit is actualizing the vision of Multibox with dexterity and devotion. Following the footsteps of his role model and mentor, his father Sri Murari Lal Agarwalla, this young entrepreneur is now heading strong in his entrepreneurial journey, learning from his experiences, the environment around, and under the tutelage of professionals. A keen observer and learner, Mohit had been responsible for setting up the entire business right from the inception stage to setting up the plant. He had invested significant time and energy in acquiring firsthand business exposure in his area of operation during his internship with leading players in the field of packaging industry.

Mr. Shantanu Agarwalla, Director (Finance & Business Development)

An Associate Member of The Institute of Chartered Accoutants of India (ICAI), Mr. Shantanu Agarwalla is a Chartered Accountant with modern unconventional innovative thinking. True to his calling Shantanu has been responsible for business development and has significant contribution right from the DPR stage of Multibox Paper and Pack. Commerce and Finance are two core domain areas of his KRAs including statutory compliances. His decision to pursue the extremely grueling and rigorous professional Chartered Accountant degree was inspired by his Father. During the course he imbibed the required business acumen and inter-personal skills like hard work, patience, perseverance, and time management. His practical training for 3 years under a practicing CA helped him acquire both academic knowledge and practical experience of the emerging business.
