About Us

Company Profile

Multibox Pvt Ltd is a professionally managed corrugated sheets & box manufacturing company. Continuing the legacy of long standing business family ethics and vast management experience, Multibox Pvt ltd has set-up state-of-the-art plant with most contemporary machine and equipments.

Situated at a conveniently placed industrial belt in NCR Region at Sonipat, Haryana Situated at a conveniently placed industrial belt in Nani Industrial Area Prayagraj. Multibox Pvt Ltd is a 100% privately owned company. Multibox ensures total commitment on the part of owner-managers which translates into direct tangible gains like high quality and on time delivery and intangible gains like personal attention and satisfactory customer relationship. Mutual trust is the mainstay of our business strategy.

Our HR Policy

Our HR Policy is driven by the philosophy of “People before Profit“. As such industry best labour and industrial relation practices with full facilities form an integral part of our business management.

STRENGTHS & ACHIEVEMENTS

Having set-up a state-of-the-art most modern plant with futuristic features such as automatic manufacturing machinery and solar power, Multibox has set a benchmark in business. Core strengths are technical and mechanical superiority, multiple product lines, quality policy, human relation policy, business affiliations and qualified manpower with very strong focus on People before Profit makes Multibox stand out in the field.

We are ISO 9001 : 2015 certified company.

Business Policy driven by spirit of 3Cs (co-operation, collaboration, commitment), Clients are encouraged to share and discuss their needs and requirements so as to secure highest efficiency and effectiveness for maximum consumer satisfaction.
