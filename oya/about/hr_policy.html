HR Policy

Our HR Policy

Our HR Policy is driven by the philosophy of “People before Profit“. As such industry best labour and industrial relation practices with full facilities form an integral part of our business management.

  • We employ best practices in managing our business, continually improving all our processes aiming to attain business excellence.
  • Integrity and honesty of our staff and workers is never in question. Business ethics are never compromised.
  • Teamwork and employee empowerment is fundamental to our culture. We respect value input and opinion and also encourage innovation and creativity. Health and Safety of our workforce is a priority for us.
  • Quality of our work is at par with international standards. Safe working practices are adopted in what ever we do.
