Company Profile

Multibox is a professionally managed corrugated sheets & box manufacturing company. Continuing the legacy of long standing business family ethics and vast management experience, Multibox Paper & Pack has set-up state-of-the-art plant with most contemporary machine and equipments.

Our HR Policy

Our HR Policy is driven by the philosophy of “People before Profit“. As such industry best labour and industrial relation practices with full facilities form an integral part of our business management.

STRENGTHS & ACHIEVEMENTS

Having set-up a state-of-the-art most modern plant with futuristic features such as automatic manufacturing machinery and solar power, Multibox has set a benchmark in business.
We are ISO 9001 : 2015 certified company.
